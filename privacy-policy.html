01. IDENTITY DISPATCH

Data Collection

Upon authentication with the Bureau, we collect specific identity markers including your Member Reference (Email) and Biometric Signatures where applicable. This is strictly for session establishment and vault security.

We do not track external browsing history. Your movements within the Chronicle remain private, encrypted within our local ledger.

02. UTILIZATION LOGS

How We Use Data

Data utilization is restricted to the following operational parameters:

  • / Session persistence and vault access.
  • / Distribution of the weekly High-Frequency Narrative.
  • / Maintenance of the global architectural index.
03. CRYPTOGRAPHIC INTEGRITY

Security Architecture

The Chronicle utilizes Advanced Encryption Standard (AES-256) for all archived data. Member credentials are never stored in plain-text. Our servers are distributed across three secure zones to ensure architectural redundancy.

04. SOVEREIGN RIGHTS

Member Sovereignty

You retain full sovereignty over your data ledger. At any time, you may request a Complete Purge of your identity reference from our archives. This action is irreversible and will revoke all Vault access immediately.
